What Are Wireframes?

Wire frames are simple drawings of something that you are planning to create. Wireframes help you to plan out what you want to build and how to do it. Using wireframes allows you to have a sketch of what you are trying to create. This helps you to create what you need faster, and better because you have a sketch of what you are trying to draw. Wireframes give you something that you want to base your final work on.

Git Workflow

The Git Workflow allows groups to collaborate on a project that they want to build together. Each member of your group will have their own branch to work on. This will give each member a place to work on their part without others on the same branch. Once they are done working on what they need to do, they push up their work and then create a pull request. Once they do that, they merge their pull request with the master branch. Thats where all the work from the other branhes gets merged to.
